Armored Core 6 Fires of Rubicon

Armored Core 6 Fires of rubicon was developed by Bandai and Fromsoftware
Armored Core 6 is the 16th Armored core game with no links to previous Armored Core games

The Story

The Story involves a Augmented human venturing into a planet named "Rubicon" to find out and gain a very valuable resource
The augmented human was the 4th generation of augmentation which is considered a relic compared to the higher generations
And the person who is leading the investgation is a man Handler named Walter
And he guides and assigns missions to his "Hounds" to help or intercept the other Corperations that are in the planet
The other Corperations are having a similar goal to gain the rare resource since it is a powerfull energy source
The Augmented Human firstly need a mercinary lisence to properly operate in Rubicon, but luckily Walter found ways to get one
by "borrowing" Mercinary lisences from dead mercinaries, once done
The Augmented human picked up a lisence from "Raven" and now can influence who wins the fight between the corperation
