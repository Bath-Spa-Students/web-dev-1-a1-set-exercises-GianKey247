--Hot chocolate--

Ingredients

  • Nutella (50g)
  • Milk powder (15g)
  • Hot water (200ml)

Step one

Have a cup ready to put the ingredients in.

Step Two

Put 50g Nutella, 15g Milk powder and 240ml Hot water.

Step Three

Stir well and enjoy.

--French Toast--

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2)
  • Slices of Bread (4)
  • Condensed Milk (4 TableSpoons)
  • Paprika (Optional)
  • Butter

Step 1

Get a Bowl that is wide enough to dip the face of the Bread

Put the two eggs and condensed milk into the bowl and mix well

Step 2

get the slices of bread and dip it into the egg mixture

Step 3

Put the pan on the stove on medium heat, then put butter to coat the pan

Step 4

Put the egg coated bread onto the pan and wait for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown

Step 5

once it is golden brown, put butter on the pan again before putting the bread on the other side
